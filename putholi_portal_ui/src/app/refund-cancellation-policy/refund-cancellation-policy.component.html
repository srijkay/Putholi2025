Refund and Cancellation Policy

  1. Home
  2. Refund and Cancellation Policy

Welcome to this web-site of PutholiTrust. We would like to make it public on our policy on refund and cancellation of donations received for the social cause on payment gateway as under:-

No refund/cancellation for the donated/registration amount paid by any donor/member will not be entertained, for the payments made through online payment gateway. No cash or refund of money will be allowed. If any in-kind support received by the donor/member from any where the material will be reached to the poorest of the poorer communities.

Once received the donation for a cause will not be refunded to the donor. No cancellation to be made. The donation will be used for the infrastructure needs of the government school.
