Our Philosophy

Be Transparent to everyone & ensure Donor's contribution is spent 100% to the school. No miscellaneous expenses for the trust are included from the donor's contribution.

To Provide Transparent & Dedicated Service to the society by building & maintaining the infrastructure needs of the government schools through Donor's contribution for the respective schools.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Vision : To ensure every child gets free,fair & quality education along with government support.

Our mission : Help every child in government school develop and achieve their potential during the most formative years of their lives

What We Do :Our registered charity in Tamilnadu, India, which will support only to revamp the existing government schools in tamilnadu for now. We have developed this website to bring out the transparency in developing the school and also on the funds received from all kind hearted people living locally / other states/ across world .Whatever we do, we make sure it is exactly what our government school needs at the right time, guided by the representative from the school / head teacher/ government officials and with no fuss.

Please note that this trust does not support any private or self financing or govt-aided schools within tamilnadu or anywhere else and also this trust will not respond to any request for help/support from a school which is not registered with this website.

We strongly believe that, SMALL HELP CAN MAKE BIG DIFFERENCE WHEN YOU REALLY NEED IT.

Looking forward for your support.

FAQ

  • What are the roles and responsibilities of Beneficiary and Annual member?

    Beneficiary can add school and requirements. Whereas Annual Member can manage & monitor the activities within the trust for all the requests from Beneficiary and other users within the admin system.Also there will be annual membership fee(to be decided at the beginning of financial year) to be collected from Annual Member, but Beneficiary need not pay any amount for registration.

  • What is the purpose of this website and how to utilize it?

    This website will support to develop or revamp the existing government schools within Tamilnadu preferably in the villages/towns.Public Users can register as beneficiary in the website with valid details. Once user approved, authorized beneficiary to add school and its requirements with relevant proof and also its beneficiary's responsibility to get all the required approvals to proceed with the requirement for the school added. Once this process is completed, Admin & Team will identify the right source to fulfill the requirement by raising funds from the public within the state/country/abroad and once fund collected, will complete the project and submit all the proofs with expenses and implementation proof in the system for all the donors to know where exactly the money is spent.

  • What is Track Your Money?

    When Donor's make donation to a particular school, we give them a unique tracking number / ID after every successful transaction. Using the Tracking number / ID, Donor can get know the current status/progress of the school development and after completion, they can view the Pre & Post implementation picture of the school condition along with Receipts detailing the payments made to the vendors for fulfilling the requirements.

  • Can I provide materials or things for free or no cost to schools through this trust or website?

    Yes. However still needs to go through the website, here zero amount workorder to be generated and still pre image and post image to be uploaded for everyone's reference.

  • What happens if funds are not collected from the public to the schools registered?

    If fund collection is not achievable through donations from public, trust authorities to decide if they can add any considerable amount to fulfill the requirement, if that is not possible, Trust will notify Beneficiary/DEO/CEO of school and the school contacts that requirements could not be fulfilled. There is no liability for the Trust to fulfill the needs of the school.

  • Can I add government aided schools for revamp/development of the school?

    For now, we are serving only Govt Schools. In future, we will support govt aided based on the decision made within the trust authorities.

  • Can anyone from trust or volunteers be vendors to supply the materials?

    Yes. However if the quotation is selected, from the selected amount there needs to be mandatory discount of 10% to be given.

  • How are the payments made to vendors?

    No payment is made by cash/cheque/card. All payments are done via payment processing through the system and no manual intervention is required.

  • Does this trust or website collects open donations?

    Putholi Trust never collects any Open donations, all donations are to any particular school and does not accept cash or cheque payments. All payments are to be made by online via the putholitrust.com. Any transactions through any other channel, trust will not take the responsibility.
